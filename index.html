The Program

Three reasons to partner with Impulse.

Financial Incentives

Earn cash for every Impulse unit you sell. We are committed to offering competitive commission rates so every introduction you make is a real revenue opportunity.

  • Competitive per‑unit commission

No new wiring. No technical construction. No additional training or certification. Impulse plugs into any standard 120V outlet, so you can expand your offering without rewiring the home.
